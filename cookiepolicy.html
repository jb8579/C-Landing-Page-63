What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow us to recognize your device and improve your browsing experience. Cookies can store information such as your preferences, login information, and usage patterns to help us enhance the functionality of our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • 1.

    Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the website's basic functions, including secure login, maintaining your shopping cart, and completing transactions.

  • 2.

    Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our site, so we can improve performance and design. They collect data such as how long you stay on the site and the pages you visit.

  • 3.

    Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow us to remember your preferences, such as language settings or region, to offer you a more personalized experience.

  • 4.

    Targeting Cookies: These cookies track your activity across the site and are used to show you relevant advertisements. This data is often shared with third-party advertisers to improve the accuracy of the ads you see.

  • 5.

    Third-Party Cookies: In some cases, we may allow third parties, such as advertisers or analytics services, to place cookies on your device. These cookies are used for analytics and targeted advertising purposes, and we have no control over how these cookies are used.

Managing Cookies

You have the ability to manage cookies in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. However, please note that disabling cookies, especially essential ones, may impact your ability to use certain features of our website, such as login and shopping cart functions.
